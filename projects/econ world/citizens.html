The Citizens of Soulland

Soulland is home to three distinct populations, each with unique characteristics, appearances, and roles within society. The flag of Soulland is designed to represent these three cultures: the human representing the Normies, the pawprint representing the Pacifists, and the two hearts representing the Vivacious.

The Normies

Physical Appearance

  • Features: Normal human-like features
  • Build: Average, varied body types
  • Clothing: Professional attire, business casual
  • Hair: Full head of hair, conventionally styled
  • Ethnicity: Varied, diverse representations
  • Appearance Age: Adults appear their actual age

Primary Role

  • Occupation: Office workers, administrators
  • Status: Middle class
  • Government: Mid-level bureaucracy
  • Skills: Organization, detail-orientation

Characteristics

  • Reliable and dependable
  • Detail-oriented workers
  • Community-focused
  • Share resources freely

The Pacifists

Physical Appearance

  • Head: Completely bald, smooth scalp
  • Features: Bookish, intellectual appearance
  • Eyes: Often wear glasses or spectacles
  • Build: Slender, lean physiques
  • Clothing: Scholarly robes, academic wear
  • Demeanor: Peaceful, thoughtful expression

Primary Role

  • Occupation: Farmers, agricultural workers
  • Status: Working class, essential workers
  • Specialization: Crop cultivation, livestock care
  • Values: Animal welfare, environmental stewardship

Characteristics

  • Peaceful and non-violent
  • Deep knowledge of nature
  • Strong animal welfare values
  • Hard-working and dedicated

The Vivacious

Physical Appearance

  • Age Appearance: Look like teenagers
  • Clothing: Street-style, baggy clothing
  • Fashion: Oversized hoodies, cargo pants
  • Footwear: Trendy sneakers, street shoes
  • Accessories: Trendy, contemporary items
  • Demeanor: Energetic, charismatic presence

Primary Role

  • Position: Government leaders, elite class
  • Authority: Make all major decisions
  • Military: Can be rented to other nations
  • Status: Upper class, born superior

Characteristics

  • Born with complete knowledge
  • Naturally talented at everything
  • Fast workers and efficient
  • Ambitious and charismatic
  • Natural leaders

Population Dynamics & Social Structure

Total Estimated Population: ~100,000 citizens

Population Distribution

  • Normies: ~40,000 (40%) - Administrative and office workforce
  • Pacifists: ~35,000 (35%) - Agricultural and environmental workers
  • Vivacious: ~25,000 (25%) - Elite leadership and specialized services

Immigration & Growth Patterns

  • Current immigration rate: ~20 new residents per 4 months (~60 per year)
  • Population growth: Slightly negative (-0.2% annually)
  • Immigration target: Increase through propaganda campaigns highlighting Vivacious superiority
  • Desired demographics: More working-age Pacifists to address agricultural needs

Social Integration & Values

Despite their differences, all three populations share a common set of values that define Soulland's hybrid capitalist-communist economy:

  • ✓ Resource Sharing: All groups willing to share possessions and resources freely
  • ✓ Work for Income: All participate in wage-earning employment
  • ✓ Community Welfare: Emphasis on collective good over individual accumulation
  • ✓ Specialization: Each group naturally excels in their designated role
  • ✓ Mutual Respect: Recognition of each group's essential contribution to society
