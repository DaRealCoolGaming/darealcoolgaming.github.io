Chick-Fil-A Chicken Sandwich

Marinate the chicken in pickle jusice in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.

Combine flour, powdered pickle, paprika, black pepper, chili powder, salt, and baking powder in a bowl.

In another bowl mix milk and egg.

Coat the chicken in the egg mixture then the flour breading mixture. Repeat.

Then pan fry it for 3-4 minutes per side.

Put the sandwhich together with a bun and other ingredients of your choice.

Enjoy!

Instructions Summary

  • Marinate chicken in pickle juice
  • Mix dry ingredients
  • Mix wet ingredients
  • Coat chicken in wet then dry mixture
  • Pan fry chicken for 3-4 minutes per side
  • Assemble sandwhich
  • Enjoy!
