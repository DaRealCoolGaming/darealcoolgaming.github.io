Opportunity Cost Examples

Example 1: Forest Preservation vs. Agricultural Expansion

Decision Made: Limit logging to preserve the single remaining forest

Opportunity Cost:

  • Cannot expand farmland into forested areas
  • Limited lumber for construction projects
  • Reduced raw materials for manufacturing
  • Lost short-term revenue from logging operations

Benefit Gained: Long-term ecosystem stability, food security from forest crops, biodiversity preservation, sustainable resource base for future generations

Example 2: Population Growth vs. Resource Management

Decision Made: Run immigration propaganda campaigns to attract settlers

Opportunity Cost:

  • Accelerated resource consumption
  • Infrastructure strain on existing systems
  • Increased pressure on the already-depleting forest
  • Greater agricultural demand
  • Housing and utility expansion required

Benefit Gained: Larger workforce, increased economic output, more tax revenue, reduced per-capita labor burden, global influence expansion

Example 3: Domestic Use vs. International Vivacious Rentals

Decision Made: Rent Vivacious to other countries' militaries for $1.2B annually

Opportunity Cost:

  • Reduced domestic government effectiveness
  • Smaller leadership pool for internal projects
  • Brain drain of elite talent
  • Loss of military defense capabilities
  • Reduced capacity for innovation and planning

Benefit Gained: $1.2B annual revenue, international prestige, diplomatic relationships, immigration attraction through perceived strength
